Privacy Policy

Updated: January 16, 2026

Thank you for using DidMore! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

DidMore is a completely local application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our servers. All data is securely stored on your device.

Information we collect includes:

  • Check-in items you create
  • Check-in records and mood data
  • Illustrations you upload
  • App settings preferences

2. Information Usage

Your data is only used for:

  • Displaying your check-in records within the app
  • Generating statistical charts
  • Saving your personalized settings

We will not use your data for any other purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload any data to the cloud
  • Data is not shared with third parties
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

4. Permissions

DidMore may require the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: For selecting and saving illustration images
  • Notification Permission: For check-in reminders (optional)

These permissions are only requested when you actively use related features, and you can disable them at any time in system settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • No network transmission
  • Regular security updates

6. Children's Privacy

DidMore is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app with the update date noted.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the in-app feedback feature.

Thank you again for your trust and support of DidMore!
